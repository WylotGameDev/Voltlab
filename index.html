VOLTLAB v0.4

Physics electronics sandbox. Build circuits, Create devices and experiment with unrealistic electricity

Tools

1 SelectDrag parts. Click Switch/Lever to toggle. Click Breaker to reset.
2 PlacePick part from the panel, click canvas to drop.
3 WireClick terminal dot → click another. RMB cancels.
4 DeleteClick part or wire to remove it.
5 WrenchRepair burnt/exploded components and wires.
6 TouchTouch live/hot parts. You WILL get hurt.
7 ExtinguishHold mouse on fires. Sprays CO₂ foam to extinguish.

Controls

ScrollZoom in / out (0.15× – 4×)
MMB dragPan camera
RRotate selected 90°
WCycle wire type
DELDelete selected
ESCCancel / deselect

Wire Types

CopperStandard — slight signal delay, reliable
SteelLess reliable, heats up under load
GoldUltra-fast — max 0.15A only, burns out
SuperconductorZero resistance, instant propagation
Heat PipeConducts heat to nearby parts
RopeNon-conductive, structural/decorative

Special Components v0.4

Tesla CoilWhen powered: arcs electricity to ALL nearby parts. Low-tolerance = fried. Metal/high-V = powered wirelessly. Range 200u.
ElectromagnetPULLS metal parts toward it. More current = stronger pull. Can yank parts off circuits!
Wood PartsNon-conductive but FLAMMABLE. Overheat → catch fire → spread. Use Extinguisher fast!
ExplosionsBarrel/HV explosions send nearby parts FLYING.
